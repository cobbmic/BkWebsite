About us!

Helping you when you need it the most.

Cobb Law Firm, LLC brings over two decades of legal experience to help you use federal laws to get out of debt.

Our law firm focuses exclusively on personal bankruptcy laws. The federal bankruptcy laws enable you to stop debt collectors harrassing you, keep your house and car, and get out of debt in a manageable way. The attorneys and staff at Cobb Law Firm work hard to advocate on your behalf.

Practice Area

We handle the two types of personal bankruptcy: Chapter 7 and Chapter 13. Click on one to learn more about it.
